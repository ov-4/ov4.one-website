What is ov4.one

This is a Minecraft server aimed to provide high-quality Crystal PvP Experience on modern versions. It has 2 sub-servers: NoCheatPlus on Minecraft 1.19.4, and GrimAC on Minecraft 1.21.1. It was established in Feb 2024 by _ov4, originally aiming to provide a good GrimAC experience with low latency. Over the last year, it has developed into the most popular dual-anticheat server with a large number of activative players.

Network:
China PoP: Guangzhou BGP by UCloud.cn, 200Gbps DDoS protection
Global PoP: Hong Kong BGP by Alibaba Cloud, 100Gbps DDoS protection
Connection between Guangzhou and Hong Kong: financial-level IPLC by China Unicom (3ms within the wire)

Average Latency:
China: 25ms
East Asia: 70ms
Southest Asia: 65ms
North America: 150ms
Europe: 160ms

Hardware:
CPU: Intel Core i9-14900K @ 6.00 GHz
RAM: 128GB
Storage: 2TB NVMe + 16TB SSD
IDC: UCloud Guangzhou Tier 3

GrimAC

It shares the same anticheat with 3c3u.org and 2b2t.cc, and it's part of 2b2t.org's anticheat system. GrimAC aims to prevent movement cheating.

  • Pearl Phase
  • Instant+Double break
  • Normal golden apples only
  • Piston Push
  • Cobweb
  • and more

NoCheatPlus

NoCheatPlus was once used on 2b2t.org before the major update in Aug 2023. It was one of the oldest anticheats in Minecraft, and widely considered as the best anticheat for Crystal PvP.

  • Instant break
  • Enchanted golden apples
  • Piston Push
  • and more
